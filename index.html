Bio Young Krime aka Da Ghetto Baby the inspiring underground Legend is a composer who shares writers credit and publishing with The Late great Micheal Jackson and with Young Jeezy on the Bonus track titled “Inspiration follow me” from Young Jeezy's 2006 Def Jam release Inspiration album. Da Ghetto Baby has his song titled “Ghetto Party 2” placed on the hit 2006 FX Channel “Thief” featuring Malik Yoba in 2004 he teamed up with The Queen of the West Coast Marvaless of West Coast Mafia on his single “Heavy Doses” and BushWick Bill of the Geto Boys on his Single “Military Tactics” both for His debut solo album “Product of Da Ghetto” Da Ghetto Baby has also shared the stage with some of the industry's most respected rap artist and comedians such as: 93' E-40 and the Click - Rialto Theatre, Tacoma Wa. 93' Brotha Lynch ,Dre Dog,San Quinn,J.T.
